Security Information
What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is a security process in which users provide two different authentication factors to verify their identity.

Why use Two-Factor Authentication?

2FA provides a higher level of security than password-only access. Even if your password is compromised, an attacker would still need the second factor to access your account.

How it works

When you log in, we'll send a one-time security code to your registered email address. Enter this code in addition to your password to complete the login process.

Need Assistance?

If you're having trouble with two-factor authentication, please contact our support team for help.
