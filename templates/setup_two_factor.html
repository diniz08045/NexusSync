What is Two-Factor Authentication?

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is a security process in which you provide two different authentication factors to verify your identity. With 2FA enabled:

  • You'll need to enter a verification code sent to your email in addition to your password when logging in
  • This helps prevent unauthorized access even if your password is compromised
  • You'll receive a notification whenever a login attempt is made to your account
